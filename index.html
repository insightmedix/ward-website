About the Recovery Unit

The Surgical Suite Recovery Unit at Cape Coast Teaching Hospital provides high-quality post-operative care following a wide range of surgical procedures. Our dedicated nursing team ensures every patient is closely monitored, comfortable, and well-informed throughout the recovery process.

We are committed to clinical excellence, compassionate care, and empowering patients and families with the knowledge they need for a safe and smooth recovery.

🩺

Continuous Monitoring

Vital signs, pain levels, and wound status assessed around the clock by trained nurses.

🛡️

Infection Prevention

Strict protocols prevent surgical site infections and hospital-acquired complications.

🏃

Mobility & Rehabilitation

Early guided mobilisation supports faster, safer recovery outcomes for every patient.

📖

Patient Education

Tailored education on wound care, medications, and warning signs at discharge.
