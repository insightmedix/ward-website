About the Ward

The Surgical Suite Recovery ward at Cape Coast Teaching Hospital provides specialized care for patients recovering after surgery. Our team ensures patient safety, comfort, and continuous monitoring during the critical recovery phase.

Our Services

• Post-operative monitoring
• Pain management
• Wound care
• Infection prevention
• Emotional support

Health Education

Patients are educated on medication adherence, wound care, hygiene, and recognizing early signs of complications. This helps ensure a safe and smooth recovery process.
