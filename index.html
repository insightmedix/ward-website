About the Ward

The Surgical Suite Recovery ward provides specialized post-operative care for patients recovering from surgical procedures. Our team ensures close monitoring, pain management, and safe recovery in a controlled clinical environment.

Our Services

• Post-operative monitoring
• Pain management and medication administration
• Wound care and infection prevention
• Patient stabilization and observation
• Emotional and psychological support

Patient Care Approach

We focus on patient-centered care, ensuring dignity, safety, and comfort throughout the recovery process. Our nurses work closely with surgical teams to provide continuous assessment and timely intervention.

Health Education

Patients and families are educated on post-surgical care, medication adherence, wound care, mobility, and signs of complications. Proper education promotes faster recovery and reduces hospital readmission rates.
